## Summary

**Commercial Assignment and Broadcast Scheduling Tool (CAST)** is designed to help broadcasters and advertising networks maximize revenue and audience engagement through smarter scheduling.

At its core, **CAST** combines **mathematical modeling** and **advanced heuristics** to create a scalable scheduling engine. The engine accounts for **advertiser requirements, contractual obligations, audience ratings, and regulatory constraints** to generate automated schedules that reduce conflicts, support fair placement, and maximize audience reach and profitability across available time slots.

Connected directly to real broadcast data, **CAST** uses **fast algorithms** to support **last-minute decisions and live events**, keeping schedules **efficient, compliant, and reliable**. The platform delivers measurable improvements in **efficiency, compliance, and revenue optimization**, giving networks and advertisers a clear operational advantage.
