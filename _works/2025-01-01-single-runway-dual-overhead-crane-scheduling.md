## Summary

**Single-Runway Dual Overhead Crane Scheduling Engine** solves the critical problem of coordinating two cranes operating on the same runway while preventing collisions and minimizing idle time. It combines a **dynamic programming approach** with heuristic methods to generate efficient schedules under real-world industrial constraints. The engine dynamically assigns tasks, enforces safety intervals, and adapts to operational uncertainty.

An integrated **simulation module** evaluates schedule feasibility in real time and improves robustness against disruptions. With this capability, factories and warehouses gain a powerful decision-support tool that significantly reduces task completion times and improves system throughput.
